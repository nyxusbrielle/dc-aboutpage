ABOUT THIS WEBSITE AND CREDITS

This website is dedicated to showcasing the rich tradition of Senakulo in Sariaya, Quezon. Senakulo is a religious reenactment of the Passion of Christ, performed annually during Holy Week. Through this site, we aim to educate and inform visitors about the history, significance, and cultural importance of this tradition in our community.
