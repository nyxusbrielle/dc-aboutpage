ABOUT THIS WEBSITE AND CREDITS

This digital archive is an output of the research entitled "Street Theater and Faith: A Case Study of the Senakulo Tradition in Sariaya, Quezon." It contains selected photos and videos documenting the Senakulo as a form of religious street theater. The archive aims to preserve its cultural, artistic, and devotional significance for academic use and future reference.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

The researchers extend their profound gratitude to all individuals, communities, and institutions who generously contributed their time, participation, and resources in the development of this digital archive. Your willingness to share photographs, videos, narratives, and other cultural materials played an integral role in the successful documentation and preservation of the Senakulo tradition in Sariaya, Quezon. The collective efforts have greatly enriched this project, ensuring that the cultural heritage embodied in the Senakulo will be preserved, studied, and appreciated by future generations.
